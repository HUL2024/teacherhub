Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: March 2026

Teacher-Hub is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use Teacher-Hub, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored securely and encrypted).
  • Profile information — qualifications, subjects taught, years of experience, location, and professional bio that you choose to add to your profile.
  • CV / Resume files — if you choose to upload a CV, it is stored securely in our file storage system.
  • Job application data — cover letters and application details you submit when applying for positions.
  • Transaction records — records of teaching notes you have purchased through the platform.
  • Usage data — general information about how you use the platform, such as pages visited and actions taken.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Teacher-Hub account.
  • Display your profile to schools and other users of the platform.
  • Connect teachers with relevant job opportunities.
  • Process purchases of teaching notes.
  • Send important account notifications and updates.
  • Improve the platform based on how it is used.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With schools — your public profile information (name, subjects, qualifications, location, bio) is visible to registered schools and other users browsing the teacher directory.
  • Service providers — we use trusted third-party services (including Supabase for database and authentication) to operate the platform. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Your password is never stored in plain text — it is encrypted using industry-standard methods. All data is stored using Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Teacher-Hub account and to keep it confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your information at any time via your dashboard.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for us to display your profile publicly.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at georgeet028@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

Teacher-Hub uses session storage (not cookies) to keep you logged in during your browser session. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Teacher-Hub is intended for adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at georgeet028@gmail.com.

🔒 Your data is yours. We will never sell your personal information. Our business model is built on providing value to educators — not on monetising your data.
