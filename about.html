About Teacher-Hub

Building the future of education in Liberia

Teacher-Hub is Liberia's first dedicated platform connecting qualified teachers with schools across all 15 counties. We believe every classroom deserves an excellent teacher — and every teacher deserves the tools to grow their career.

Our Mission

To eliminate the gap between qualified teachers and the schools that need them, by creating a trusted, easy-to-use platform built specifically for the Liberian education system.

We are not a generic job board. Every feature on Teacher-Hub — from the job listings to the teaching notes marketplace — has been designed with the realities of Liberian educators in mind.

What We Offer

  • Job Board — Schools post verified vacancies. Teachers apply in one click with their profile.
  • Teaching Notes Marketplace — Curriculum-aligned resources for every grade level, from Nursery to Grade 12 and beyond.
  • Teacher Directory — Schools can browse and connect with qualified teachers across Liberia.
  • Professional Profiles — Teachers build a digital CV visible to schools on the platform.

Our Story

Teacher-Hub was founded with a simple observation: great teachers in Liberia were struggling to find opportunities, while schools were struggling to find great teachers. The connection was missing.

We built Teacher-Hub to be that connection — a professional, trusted space where the Liberian education community can find each other, share resources, and grow together.

Our Values

  • Education First — Every decision we make starts with what is best for Liberian students and teachers.
  • Trust & Transparency — All schools and job listings are reviewed before going live.
  • Accessibility — We keep costs low so that teachers at every income level can benefit from the platform.
  • Community — We are building more than a product. We are building a professional community for Liberian educators.

📍 Based in Liberia — Teacher-Hub is proudly built for and by people who care deeply about Liberian education. We are committed to this community for the long term.
