Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a teacher account?

Click Sign Up as Teacher in the navigation bar or footer. Fill in your name, email, and password. You can complete your full profile — qualifications, subjects, CV — from your dashboard after signing up.

How do I post a job vacancy as a school?

Register your school using the Register School link, then log in and go to your School Dashboard. Click Post a Job and fill in the details. All job posts are reviewed by our admin team before going live — this usually takes less than 24 hours.

How do I purchase a teaching note?

Browse the Notes Marketplace, find a note you want, and click Buy & Download. Free notes can be downloaded immediately. Paid note payment integration is coming soon — contact us to arrange a purchase in the meantime.

I found a problem or bug on the site. What should I do?

Please email us at georgeet028@gmail.com with a description of the issue, the page it occurred on, and your device or browser if possible. We take all bug reports seriously and will fix issues as quickly as we can.

Is Teacher-Hub free to use?

Yes — creating a teacher or school account is completely free. Some premium features, such as featured listings and priority placement in the directory, will be available as optional upgrades. Teaching notes may have a small purchase fee set by the platform.

💡 For urgent matters — if your account has been compromised or you need immediate assistance, please email us directly at georgeet028@gmail.com with the subject line URGENT.
